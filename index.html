3. QnA

Questions you might have:

Why is this website created with pure HTML, CSS and JS?

If you don't know the pain, you can't enjoy truthfully the ease life brings you. In that line of thought, I chose to use pure code here to show my understanding with the basic languages, more than just using frameworks to fill gaps in my code and knowledge.

Where did you studied?

When I first met the world of web development I started studying with freeCodeCamp and The Odin Project, where I developed my knowledge in basic HTML, CSS, JS, GIT and the internet in general.

Do you study now?

Yes! I never stopped - nor will stop - my studies. Currently I am learning more in-depth about JavaScript, Vue.js and other technologies under HCode and Alura classes, and by doing continuous work in several projects to put to the test my skills and knowledge.

What's your skills?

As a programmer, I'm currently developing a in-depth knowledge of front-end web development, using as my main tool Vue.js and Tailwind. But I also have a good knowledge of HTML, Bootstrap, Git, GitHub, SASS, etc. In terms of soft skills, I am very communicative, disciplined, organized, I value processes and conventions (both in my codes and company processes) and I use Conventional Commits and the 5W2H method during my work process.

What's your goal?

Keep learning more and more about web development, improve continuously my skills, and become a full-stack web developer.
